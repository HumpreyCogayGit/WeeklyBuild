About Spirograph

A spirograph is a geometric drawing device that produces mathematical roulette curves called hypotrochoids and epitrochoids. The formula used:

x = (R - r) * cos(t) + p * cos((R - r) / r * t)
y = (R - r) * sin(t) - p * sin((R - r) / r * t)
